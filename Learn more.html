B.B.Y.M.C.

Bantay Bayan Youth Manila Chapter

Learn More About Us

The Bantay Bayan Youth Manila Chapter (B.B.Y.M.C.) is a youth-led organization dedicated to empowering and uniting the young people of Manila in supporting public safety, community service, and rescue operations. As a “force multiplier,” B.B.Y.M.C. collaborates closely with Bantay Bayan Manila Chapter, amplifying the impact of local emergency response and safety efforts. Together, we work on the ground to help in rescue operations, community support, and event management, engaging youth to become active participants in creating safer, more resilient communities.

B.B.Y.M.C. believes that young people have the power to make a significant difference in their communities. By training and guiding youth, we aim to prepare the next generation of leaders to take active roles in service, security, and civic duty, ensuring that the values of volunteerism, respect, and community care are passed down and strengthened.

Mission and Vision

Mission: B.B.Y.M.C. is committed to uniting and empowering the youth of Manila to actively participate in community safety and rescue operations. Our mission is to enhance the efficiency and reach of local emergency response teams by providing additional support as a “force multiplier.” Through this, we promote a culture of volunteerism, disaster preparedness, and resilience among young people. We aim to provide opportunities for youth to develop leadership skills, foster a sense of duty, and work together to build stronger communities.

Vision: Our vision is to see a safer, more resilient Manila, where empowered youth leaders actively contribute to community protection, disaster response, and civic responsibility. We envision a future where every barangay is a safer place because of the proactive involvement of young individuals who are equipped, trained, and ready to step up as agents of positive change. We strive to cultivate a generation of responsible, motivated youth who take pride in protecting and improving their communities.

Programs and Activities

Rescue Operations Support: B.B.Y.M.C. participates in rescue operations, where we provide assistance during natural disasters, emergencies, and other critical incidents. Our members are trained to support emergency responders by offering logistical help, crowd control, and on-the-ground coordination.

Community Escorting: In events and activities like Simbang Gabi (a series of pre-Christmas dawn masses) or large gatherings, B.B.Y.M.C. volunteers help escort attendees and ensure safety by assisting with crowd management and providing directions or aid when necessary.

Event Documentation: We prioritize documenting our events and activities to both reflect on our impact and to inspire other young people to get involved. From photos to written records, we make sure every effort is well-documented and recognized.

Youth Empowerment Initiatives: We encourage youth to be active in various forms of community support. From simple acts of kindness to organized events, we help them see the value in serving their community in any way they can.

Our Impact on Youth

A central goal of B.B.Y.M.C. is to inspire the youth to make positive choices that keep them from risky or rebellious behaviors. We focus on providing mentorship, leadership training, and active engagement to guide young people toward fulfilling and meaningful contributions to society. Through B.B.Y.M.C., we aim to create a strong sense of purpose and belonging, deterring youth from engaging in crime or harmful activities by channeling their energy into productive community involvement.

Get Involved

Volunteer: Join us as a volunteer to be part of rescue operations, safety patrols, or community outreach programs. B.B.Y.M.C. welcomes all youth who are interested in helping others, learning new skills, and becoming active members of their communities.

Partner With Us: If you’re part of an organization or community group, you can partner with B.B.Y.M.C. to co-host events, support public safety initiatives, or join our youth empowerment efforts.

Participate in Our Events: Attend and participate in our activities to see what B.B.Y.M.C. is all about. From trainings to outreach events, there are many ways to get involved and learn more.
